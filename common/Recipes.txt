Recipes
-------

This text file will list all 
the recipes this mod adds.

Unless other wise stated, all
recipes are recognized as forwards
and backwards

-> one way

= Both ways

Vanilla
-------

8 Glass Panes -> 3 Glass Blocks
1 Coal/CharCoal = 2 Redstone
7 Coal/CharCoal = 1 Lapis Lazuli
8 Gold Ingots = 1 Emerald

IC2
---

1 Sticky Resin = 1 Leather
3 Copper Ingots = 1 Tin Ingot
2 Tin Ingots = 2 Bronze Ingot
2 Uranium -> 1 Diamond
2 Diamond -> 4 Uranium
7 Bronze = 1 Uranium
2 Gold -> 1 Uranium

Thermal Expansion
-----------------

3 Copper Ingots = 1 Tin Ingot
1 Silver Ingot = 2 Lead Ingots

Forestry
--------

3 Copper Ingots = 1 Tin Ingot
2 Tin Ingots = 2 Bronze Ingots

Mechro Magiks
-------------

3 Copper Ingots = 1 Tin Ingot

RailCraft
---------

4 Coal/CharCoal = 1 Coal Coke

If IC2 Installed: 2 Steel = 2 Reinforced Iron


ThaumCraft 3
------------

1 Wind Shard -> 1 Fire Shard -> 1 Water Shard -> 1 Earth Shard -> 1 Wind Shard
3 Dull Shards = 1 Vis Shard

Markers/Candles
White -> Orange -> Magenta -> Light Blue -> Yellow -> Lime Green -> Pink -> Gray 
-> Light Gray -> Cyan -> Purple -> Blue -> Brown -> Green -> Red -> Black -> White

Applied Energistics
-------------------

4 Quartz Dust = 4 Quartz Crystal
2 Iron Ingot = 1 Quartz Crystal
Cables:
Blue -> Black -> White -> Brown -> Red -> Yellow -> Green -> Blue
